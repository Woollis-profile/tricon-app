🏋️ TriCon 3-3-3 Training Program

Over 50s Strength Training | 2-Day Split | 60 Minutes Per Session

What is TriCon 3-3-3?

TriCon training combines three contraction types in sequence to maximize muscle engagement and strength gains while being joint-friendly for mature athletes.

1
Eccentric

3 seconds lowering (negative phase)

2
Isometric

3 seconds hold at bottom

3
Concentric

3 seconds lifting (positive phase)

Each rep takes 9 seconds total. Perform 3 sets of 6-8 reps per exercise with 90-120 seconds rest between sets.

MONDAY

Upper Body Focus

🔥 Warm-Up (8 minutes)

1. Arm Circles & Shoulder Rolls
2 minutes - Forward and backward, 10 each direction
2. Light Resistance Band Work
3 minutes - Pull-aparts, rows, external rotations
3. Incline Walking
3 minutes - Treadmill, moderate pace to elevate heart rate

💪 Main Workout (45 minutes)

1. Chest Press (Machine or Dumbbells)
3 sets × 6-8 reps | 3-3-3 tempo
Rest: 90-120 seconds between sets
2. Seated Cable Row
3 sets × 6-8 reps | 3-3-3 tempo
Rest: 90-120 seconds between sets
3. Shoulder Press (Machine)
3 sets × 6-8 reps | 3-3-3 tempo
Rest: 90-120 seconds between sets
4. Lat Pulldown
3 sets × 6-8 reps | 3-3-3 tempo
Rest: 90-120 seconds between sets
5. Bicep Curl (Cable or Dumbbells)
2 sets × 8 reps | 3-3-3 tempo
Rest: 60 seconds between sets
6. Tricep Pushdown (Cable)
2 sets × 8 reps | 3-3-3 tempo
Rest: 60 seconds between sets

❄️ Cool-Down (7 minutes)

1. Static Stretching
5 minutes - Chest, shoulders, triceps, lats (30 seconds each)
2. Deep Breathing
2 minutes - Box breathing for recovery

WEDNESDAY

Lower Body Focus

🔥 Warm-Up (8 minutes)

1. Hip Circles & Leg Swings
2 minutes - 10 circles each direction, 10 swings each leg
2. Bodyweight Squats
2 minutes - 15-20 reps, focus on form and range of motion
3. Elliptical or Bike
4 minutes - Low resistance, warm up joints and muscles

💪 Main Workout (45 minutes)

1. Leg Press
3 sets × 6-8 reps | 3-3-3 tempo
Rest: 120 seconds between sets
2. Romanian Deadlift (Dumbbells)
3 sets × 6-8 reps | 3-3-3 tempo
Rest: 120 seconds between sets
3. Leg Curl (Machine)
3 sets × 8 reps | 3-3-3 tempo
Rest: 90 seconds between sets
4. Leg Extension (Machine)
3 sets × 8 reps | 3-3-3 tempo
Rest: 90 seconds between sets
5. Standing Calf Raise
3 sets × 10 reps | 3-3-3 tempo
Rest: 60 seconds between sets
6. Plank Hold
2 sets × 30-45 seconds
Rest: 60 seconds between sets

❄️ Cool-Down (7 minutes)

1. Static Stretching
5 minutes - Quads, hamstrings, glutes, calves, hip flexors (30 seconds each)
2. Foam Rolling
2 minutes - IT band, quads, calves for Saturday run prep

⚠️ Important Training Notes for Over 50s

  • Start Conservative: Begin with lighter weights to master the 3-3-3 tempo. Form is everything.
  • Listen to Your Body: Joint discomfort? Reduce range of motion or weight. Muscle fatigue is good; joint pain is not.
  • Hydration: Drink water throughout your session, especially important for mature athletes.
  • Recovery: Wednesday's lower body session is strategically timed to allow 3 days recovery before your Saturday long run.
  • Progression: Add weight only when you can complete all sets with perfect form. Progress slowly—2.5-5 lbs per week maximum.
  • Rest Days: Tuesday, Thursday, Friday are crucial for adaptation and recovery. Light walking is encouraged.
