The TRICON System

THREE DISCIPLINES.
THREE SETS. NINE REPS.

A simple, repeatable framework designed for progressive strength over the long term — not a 12-week programme, a permanent practice.

3

Monday is upper body. Wednesday is lower body. Friday is kettlebell. Each session follows the same proven structure — add weight when the reps feel easy, hold the load when you need recovery, and let your body set the pace. No guesswork. No complexity. Just show up and lift.
