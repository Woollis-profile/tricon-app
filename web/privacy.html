Privacy Policy

This page outlines how TriCon Training handles personal information, workout data, and account details in line with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles.

Last updated: June 2026 Australian Privacy Act Supabase + App Store

1. Overview

Overview

TriCon Training is operated by Grant Harvey. We are committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

2. Data We Collect

Data we collect

We collect the following information to operate the app and support account syncing:

  • Email address
  • Encrypted password
  • Workout sessions (exercise type, duration, weight, sets, reps, date)
  • App settings (unit preference, training week, kettlebell weight, push-up maximum)

We do not collect your name, phone number, or location.

3. How We Use Your Data

How we use your data

We use your data to authenticate your account, sync workout history and settings across devices, and display session history and training stats. We do not use your information for advertising or analytics profiling.

4. Data Storage

Storage and security

Data is stored securely via Supabase (supabase.com), encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest, and hosted on AWS.

5. Data Sharing

Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data. Third parties are limited to:

  • Supabase for database and authentication services
  • RevenueCat for purchase verification only
  • Apple App Store for payment processing

6. Data Retention

Retention

We retain your data while your account remains active. If you request deletion, we will fully remove your data within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

Your rights

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles, you may request access to your data, request correction, request deletion, and lodge a complaint with the OAIC at oaic.gov.au.

8. Children

Children

TriCon Training is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13.

9. Changes

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes are made constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
